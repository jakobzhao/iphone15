# Top 10 Cities in the U.S.

This README highlights the top 10 most populous cities in the United States and gives a short description of what each place is known for.

## 1. New York City, New York
The largest city in the country, New York City is a global center for finance, media, fashion, and culture. It is known for landmarks such as Times Square, Central Park, and the Statue of Liberty.

## 2. Los Angeles, California
Los Angeles is famous for the entertainment industry, especially Hollywood. It also has major influence in music, technology, trade, and international culture.

## 3. Chicago, Illinois
Chicago is one of the most important cities in the Midwest. It is known for its architecture, deep-dish pizza, museums, sports teams, and strong business and transportation sectors.

## 4. Houston, Texas
Houston is a major hub for energy, medicine, and space exploration. It is home to the Texas Medical Center and NASA's Johnson Space Center.

## 5. Phoenix, Arizona
Phoenix is one of the fastest-growing large cities in the country. It is known for its desert climate, expanding economy, and proximity to outdoor recreation.

## 6. Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
Philadelphia played a central role in early American history. It is known for Independence Hall, the Liberty Bell, leading universities, and a strong healthcare sector.

## 7. San Antonio, Texas
San Antonio is known for its rich Spanish and Mexican heritage. Popular attractions include the Alamo and the River Walk, and the city has a growing military and healthcare presence.

## 8. San Diego, California
San Diego is known for its mild weather, beaches, military bases, and biotechnology industry. It is also a major destination for tourism and outdoor activities.

## 9. Dallas, Texas
Dallas is a major business and technology center in the South. It has a strong economy supported by finance, telecommunications, transportation, and real estate.

## 10. Jacksonville, Florida
Jacksonville is the largest city by land area in the contiguous United States among incorporated cities. It is known for its port activity, riverfront, beaches, and growing financial services sector.

## Notes

- This list refers to the 10 most populous incorporated cities in the United States.
- Rankings can shift slightly over time depending on new census estimates.
